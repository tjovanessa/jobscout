PT. Royal Trust

Jl. Jendral Sudirman No. 86, Surabaya 10220

PT. Royal Trust Futures was founded in 2005 and has been officially licensed as a Futures Broker and supervised by the Commodity Futures Trading Supervisory Agency (BAPPEBTI) since 2006, being a member of PT. Jakarta Futures Exchange (BBJ) since 2005 and is a member of PT. Indonesian Futures Clearing (KBI) since 2012. PT. Royal Trust Futures which operates in the field of Multilateral Futures Trading and other Derivatives, has traded products including; London Loco Gold, Foreign Exchange (Forex), and Crude Oil.

Providing services and information on commodity futures trading through a system that is transparent, credible, trustworthy and has integrity.
